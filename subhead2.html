    Yu-Gi-Oh & Yu-Gi-Oh Duel Monster

    Yu-Gi-Oh

    Yu-Gi-Oh the Tv Anime Series (1998) not to be confused with Yu-Gi-Oh Duel Monster Tv Anime series (2000) follows the original manga adaptation, the story is much darker with focus on a cast of charaters more than dueling with monsters. It is also called Season 0 by fans, each episode will revolve around Yugi and his group of friends, when conflicts arises Yami Yugi will take over and challenge the person for that episode, he explains the rules of the Penalty Game and participate. It ends with the person losing because they were too greedy or tried to cheat the game, in the end Yami Yugi was able to reveal their true self and punish them within their mind.

    Yugioh Duel Monsters

    This anime series is the most well recognized within online spheres, contains multiple story arcs and while it will be jarring that the first episode assumed you knew about Yugi's friend group, the story is not hard to follow if you're watching the 4Kids! dub. The premise starts out with Yugi getting roped into battles, and later it follows the manga with the story arcs.

    You can watch of all of season 1 here

    Yu-Gi-Oh GX

    Sequel to Yugioh 10 years later, follows a new protagonist named Jaden Yuki who is on his way to the entrance exam at Duel Academy. He bumps into Yugi Muto who gave him a Kuriboh Monster card claiming that he has a strong bond with his cards. The entrance exam at duel academy tests the students to see if they are ready to become duelist and for Jaden, he was able to overcome an unfair duel with the professor and marked his spot within the academy.

    You can watch GX here

    Yu-Gi-Oh 5DS

    Yugioh 5DS takes place in a new city called Domino City where there is clear discrepancy between the class, this time dueling is paired with high speed motorcycle. This anime introduces a new protagonist, Yusei Fudo, in the first episode a friend of his turned rival, Jack Atlas, decided to steal his very specific card and ran away *gasp*. Instead of dealing with his stolen card, the first episode showcases how the battles works with the high speed motorcycle with a random thug that decided to fight him. Now Yusei can deal with his stolen card matter.
